03 — How it works
How it works

Calls in. Insights out. Live or after the fact.

CallGuard sits alongside your existing dialer or telephony stack. Stream audio live as the call is happening or push recordings after the fact. Either way: transcript, score, breaches, coaching — all surfaced inside our dashboard or fed back into your tooling via webhook and API.

1

Ingest — live or batch

Drag-and-drop, REST API, SFTP/S3 sync for recordings, or live WebSocket streaming from Twilio Media Streams, AWS Connect, Vonage, Plivo, Telnyx or any WebRTC-capable dialer (generic adapter). Older Genesys / Avaya stacks via SIPREC. WAV, MP3, OGG, WebM, M4A, μ-law, Opus all supported. Encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM.

2

Score — as it happens

Deepgram transcribes live with speaker separation. Claude scores against your scorecard, factoring in your firm's knowledge base, prior corrections and exemplar calls. Mid-call breach detection runs every 30 seconds on the rolling transcript.

3

Act — before the call ends

Critical breaches fire instantly to email, Slack, or your supervisor's phone — and stream back via signed webhooks to your dialer or CRM. Pass/fail per criterion, weighted overall score, evidence quotes and agent coaching draft are ready the moment the call ends.

Scorecards built for any vertical

Build your scorecard from scratch in our editor or upload your existing CSV. Every item is configurable: pass/fail, 1–5 scale, 1–10 scale, weighted critically, severity-tagged.

📞

Sales & service scorecards

Greeting, identity check, empathy markers, value pitch, objection handling, no-pressure language, consent capture, summary close.

🛡️

Compliance scorecards

TPS / DNC, recording disclosure, mini-Miranda, GDPR / CCPA references, vulnerable customer screening, multi-tenant per-client scorecards for BPOs.

05 — Live & Embedded
Live & Embedded

Score the call while it's still happening.

Most QA tools wait until a call ends. CallGuard streams audio live from your dialer, transcribes in real time, and flags critical compliance breaches mid-call — before the rep finishes the pitch. Live transcript builds as it's spoken; every 30 seconds the AI reviews what's been said and emits high-confidence breach alerts via signed webhook to your CRM, dialer or supervisor's screen.

How the live engine is wired

📡

Dialer streaming

First-class adapters for Twilio Media Streams and AWS Connect Voice Streams. Vonage, Plivo, Telnyx and any WebRTC dialer supported via our generic protocol. Older Genesys / Avaya stacks via SIPREC.

🌐

Browser audio streaming

Any modern browser can stream audio over the same WebSocket protocol — useful for soft-phone agent desktops, embedded customer-facing forms, and reference clients dropped into your own web app without writing the streaming layer yourself.

🔔

Mid-call breach webhooks

HMAC-SHA256 signed POST to your endpoint within 30 seconds of detection. Retry with exponential backoff. Full audit log of every delivery attempt visible in your dashboard.

🎯

Confidence-gated alerts

Live scoring only emits a breach when the AI is >75% confident the criterion has already been broken — not "might be" or "could happen." Designed to avoid alert fatigue from day one.

🛡️

Consent capture, audited

The streaming protocol takes a verbal-yes consent.captured event before scoring proceeds. Consent timestamps and excerpts are stored with the call and surfaced in the dashboard, giving you a GDPR Article 6 evidence trail without writing your own logic.

🧠

AI that learns your bar

Every score correction your compliance officer makes is fed back into future prompts. After 50–100 corrections the AI calibrates to your firm's interpretation of "fair value," "vulnerable," and "good outcome" — not a generic baseline that drifts.

03 — How it works
How it works

Calls in. Suitability evidence out.

CallGuard sits alongside your existing recording infrastructure. Whether your advisers record on a desk dialer, on Teams, or via a dedicated compliance recorder, audio flows in, gets scored against your firm's scorecard, and surfaces in a dashboard your compliance director actually wants to open.

1

Ingest — live or batch

Drag-and-drop, REST API, SFTP/S3 sync for archived recordings, or live WebSocket streaming from a Twilio, AWS Connect or compliance recorder. Encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM.

2

Score against your scorecard

Deepgram transcribes with speaker separation. Claude scores against your firm's scorecard, factoring in your KB (firm policies, product cheatsheets, FCA references), prior compliance corrections, and exemplar calls.

3

Surface what matters

Pass/fail per criterion, weighted overall score, exact quote evidence, an adviser-specific coaching brief, and a breach register. Critical fails alert your compliance lead instantly.

Scoring built around the FCA framework

📋

Suitability (COBS 9.2)

ATR explored properly (not just a closed question), capacity for loss assessed, time horizon confirmed, existing arrangements reviewed, recommendation linked back to stated objectives.

🛡️

Vulnerable customers (FG21/1)

Indicators screened for: bereavement, cognitive impairment, financial distress, time pressure. Vulnerability flag triggers the firm's protocol; absence of screening is itself a flag.

💷

Charges & Consumer Duty

Combined monetary illustration (not just %), product charges, ongoing service fees, fair value test, plain-English explanation, customer asked to confirm understanding.

🚨

Critical fails (regulator-relevant)

Urgency / pressure language ("offer closes today"), unauthorised pension transfer steers without specialist sign-off, missing recording disclosure, undisclosed conflicts of interest.

04 — Platform & AI layer
Recent build

Score the call while the adviser is still on it.

The shift in the last month is platform-level. CallGuard isn't a "drag-and-drop your recordings" tool any more. The AI reads the call as it's happening, calibrates itself to your compliance team's interpretation, and writes the brief your compliance director would otherwise spend a Sunday afternoon producing.

📡

Live streaming + mid-call breach detection

Audio streams live from your dialer or compliance recorder. Every 30 seconds the AI scans the rolling transcript and flags critical breaches via signed webhook to your supervisor. The compliance lead can step in during the call, not three weeks after.

🎯

Confidence-gated alerts

The live scorer only emits a breach when the AI is >75% confident the criterion has clearly already failed. Designed to avoid alert fatigue and the "boy who cried wolf" problem from day one.

🧠

Per-firm AI learning

Your compliance officer corrects the AI on borderline calls. Each correction becomes a few-shot example feeding the next batch of scoring. After ~50-100 corrections the AI is calibrated to your firm's interpretation of "suitable" and "fair value," not a generic baseline.

⭐

Firm exemplar library

Mark gold-standard advice calls. The AI uses them as the reference bar when scoring future calls — your firm's "good" defines the AI's "good," not someone else's.

📊

AI Insights — written for your CO

A weekly strategic brief: what changed this week, the patterns worth attention, and 3-4 specific actions ("escalate Adviser X this week, run a team-wide refresher on capacity for loss, update the charges script to include £ illustration"). Written by Claude, not boilerplate.

🤝

Adviser coaching memory

Coaching builds on what was said to that adviser last week. If they've improved on the flagged area, the AI acknowledges it. If they haven't, it escalates the language. Personalised, not template.

What's coming next: live in-call coaching

Beyond breach detection: a real-time AI coach that whispers context-aware nudges to the adviser mid-call ("ask about capacity for loss now," "soften the close") via earpiece or on-screen prompt. Personalised to each adviser using the prior coaching memory above. No legacy QA vendor delivers this on modern LLMs.

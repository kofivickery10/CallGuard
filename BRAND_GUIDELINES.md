# CallGuard AI Brand Guidelines

## Brand Identity

**Tagline:** Every sales conversation. Scored, live, by AI that learns your firm's bar.

**Personality:** The Expert Analyst - calm, precise, trustworthy.

**Wordmark:** "CallGuard AI" — the "AI" rendered in primary sage (#4a9e6e) on light backgrounds, white at 92% opacity on dark backgrounds.

**Logo mark:** Three ascending audio bars plus an AI-pulse dot (top-right), rendered in white on a sage circle (primary use) or sage on a white circle (sales-pack covers).

## Color Palette

| Token | Hex | Usage |
|-------|-----|-------|

| Primary hover | `#3d8a5e` | Hover states |
| Primary light | `#e8f0e8` | Active nav background, light highlights |

## Typography

**Font:** Inter (single font family)
- Page title: 22px, weight 700, letter-spacing -0.3px
- Card label: 12px, weight 600, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.5px
- Card value: 30px, weight 700
- Table header: 11px, uppercase, letter-spacing 0.5px, weight 600
- Table cell: 13px
- Nav item: 14px, weight 500
- Badge: 11px, weight 600

## Components

- **Sidebar:** White bg, 220px fixed, 1px right border, green circle logo
- **Cards:** White, 1px border, 10px radius, 20px padding
- **Buttons:** Primary green or outline, 8px radius, 13px font
- **Badges:** Pill-shaped (20px radius), semantic colors
- **Score bars:** 50px wide, 5px tall, colored by score range
- **Tables:** Light header bg, uppercase labels, hoverable rows
- **Upload zone:** Dashed border, 12px radius, centered icon

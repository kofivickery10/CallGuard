Pricing FAQ

Common questions, answered.

What counts as a seat?

A seat is an agent or adviser whose calls CallGuard AI scores. Compliance officers, supervisors and viewers who review results are not charged as seats.

Are there volume discounts?

Yes. The £199 figure is the starting point for smaller teams; per-seat pricing steps down as seat count grows. Tell us your team size and we will quote the exact rate.

Is there a minimum contract term?

No multi-year minimum is required. Annual billing attracts a discount, but you can start monthly and move to annual once the product has proved itself on your own calls.

Does the price include transcription and AI scoring?

Yes. Transcription, redaction, AI scoring and coaching drafts are all part of the plan; there is no separate per-minute transcription bill.

Can I trial it before committing?

Book a 15-minute demo and bring five of your own call recordings; we will score them against your scorecard live, on your data. There is no 90-day pilot, no solution architects and no integration project; sign up in the morning and have 100 calls scored before lunch.

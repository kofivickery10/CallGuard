AI call QA for Five9.

First-class adapter for Five9's VoiceStream API. Live transcription, scoring against your scorecard, mid-call breach detection within 30 seconds, and HMAC-signed webhooks back to your CRM, agent desktop or supervisor dashboard. Five9 Quality Management complemented, not replaced.

How the integration works

Two patterns: VoiceStream live or recording-API batch.

Five9 publishes live audio over the VoiceStream API, accessible from your tenant via OAuth. CallGuard's adapter consumes the stream natively and scores in real time. For environments where streaming is not viable, we use the Five9 recording API to pull post-call audio for batch scoring.

1

Authorise CallGuard in Five9

Add CallGuard AI as a permitted application in your Five9 tenant. Grant scopes for VoiceStream and the recording API. Standard Five9 OAuth flow, takes minutes.

2

We score in real time

Audio frames consumed natively from VoiceStream. Live transcription with speaker separation. Claude scores the rolling transcript against your scorecard every 30 seconds, looking for high-confidence breaches at over 75% threshold.

3

Webhooks fire back

HMAC-signed POST to your endpoint within 30 seconds of detection, plus a final scored event when the call ends. Compatible with Five9's IVA / Studio webhooks so you can trigger Studio scripts from a breach event (open a supervisor task, escalate, route the next call).

What you get

Built for the way Five9 customers actually deploy.

VoiceStream-native frame parsing

Our Five9 adapter parses the VoiceStream protocol directly. No intermediary container, no audio re-encoding, no Lambda bridge required. Five9 conversation IDs flow through to CallGuard's external_id field for record matching.

30-second breach cadence

Mid-call breach detection runs every 30 seconds against the rolling transcript. Only emits a breach when the AI is over 75% confident a criterion has clearly already failed. No alert fatigue.

HMAC-signed webhooks into Five9 Studio

Webhooks land at any endpoint Five9 can call via Studio actions. Open a supervisor task, escalate a contact, fire a Slack message, route the next call from this agent to a senior queue. All triggered automatically by the AI's breach detection.

Five9 Quality Management compatible

Per-call scoring results can flow into Five9 QM evaluations, calibration reports and agent scorecards via the Five9 API. Use CallGuard's AI scoring to populate Five9's QM workflow rather than replacing it.

Per-tenant AI calibration

Your supervisors correct AI scores in CallGuard. Corrections feed back into future scoring so the AI calibrates to your interpretation of "fair value", "vulnerable", and "good outcome", not a generic baseline.

Encryption and residency

Audio (if retained) is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Hosted in eu-west-2 by default; us-east-1 and ap-southeast-2 available for US / APAC customers. UK GDPR Article 28 DPA published.

Five9 integration FAQ

Common questions, answered.

Does this replace Five9 Quality Management?

Optionally. Many customers use CallGuard AI as the scoring engine and Five9 QM as the evaluation / calibration / coaching workflow on top. Others replace QM entirely. Both patterns work depending on whether your team has invested in QM workflows or not.

How long does the integration take?

Typically 2 to 3 days from OAuth authorisation to first scored call, including scorecard upload and a sample-data sanity check. Faster if you start with the recording API (batch) and add VoiceStream (live) later.

Does this work with Five9's Intelligent Virtual Agent (IVA) / IVA Studio?

CallGuard AI scores the human-agent portion of calls, not the IVA self-service portion (where there is no agent to score). Calls that escalate from IVA to human are scored from the moment the human picks up. IVA Studio can be triggered from CallGuard webhooks (e.g., to route the next call from a flagged agent to a senior queue).

Do calls from Five9's outbound predictive dialler work the same?

Yes. Predictive, progressive and preview campaigns all flow through VoiceStream. The scoring is identical. We also score outbound-specific compliance items (TPS / TCPA / mini-Miranda) on these calls. More on outbound scoring here.

What about data residency for UK / EU Five9 deployments?

Default eu-west-2 (London region) on AWS. Calls audio (if retained) and transcripts stay in the chosen region. Five9 customers operating in UK / EU markets typically run on this default. Other regions available for US / APAC tenants.

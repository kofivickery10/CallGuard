Sub-processors

Last updated: 8 June 2026 · Version: 1.1

This page lists the third-party providers ("Sub-processors") that CallGuard AI uses to deliver the Services. Each of these Sub-processors may Process Customer Personal Data on our behalf. We have entered into a written data-protection agreement with each Sub-processor that contains obligations no less protective than those set out in our Data Processing Agreement.

We notify customers of any addition or replacement of Sub-processors at least 30 days before the change takes effect. Customers may subscribe to updates by emailing privacy@callguardai.co.uk.

Reading this page: we group Sub-processors by purpose so customers can quickly assess data-protection risk for their use case. Where a Sub-processor is headquartered or processes data outside the UK, we identify the transfer safeguard that applies.

Customer-configured integrations

Where a customer chooses to integrate the Services with a third-party platform of their own (e.g., Twilio, AWS Connect, Vonage for live audio streaming; their own CRM or webhook endpoint), that third party is acting on the customer's instruction, not on ours. CallGuard AI does not enter into a Sub-processor relationship with these third parties on the customer's behalf. The customer is responsible for any DPAs or transfer safeguards required between themselves and those parties.

How we update this list

When we add or replace a Sub-processor:

  • We update this page with the new entry and the effective date;
  • We email all active customers at least 30 days before the change takes effect;
  • Customers may object on reasonable data-protection grounds within 14 days of notification, in which case we will work to find an alternative or, failing that, the customer may terminate the affected Service on the terms set out in the DPA.

To subscribe to changes by email, send a request to privacy@callguardai.co.uk.

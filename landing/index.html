How it works

Calls in. Compliance evidence out.

CallGuard AI sits alongside your existing telephony or recording infrastructure. Whether your team uses a desk dialer, a compliance recorder, Microsoft Teams, or simply uploads recorded advice files, audio flows in, gets scored against your firm's scorecard, and surfaces in a dashboard your compliance director actually wants to open.

1

Ingest, live or batch

Drag-and-drop, REST API, SFTP / S3 sync for archived recordings, or live WebSocket streaming from Twilio, AWS Connect, Vonage, Plivo or any WebRTC-capable dialer. Encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM.

2

Score, as it happens

Best-in-class speech recognition produces a live transcript with speaker separation. Claude scores against your firm's scorecard, factoring in your knowledge base, prior compliance corrections, and exemplar calls. Mid-call breach detection runs every 30 seconds on the rolling transcript.

3

Act, before the call ends

Pass / fail per criterion, weighted overall score, exact quote evidence, an agent-specific coaching brief. Critical breaches fire instantly to email, Slack or your supervisor's screen, and stream back via signed webhooks to your CRM or dialer.

Live & embedded

Score the call while it's still happening.

Most QA tools wait until a call is over. CallGuard AI streams audio live from your dialer or compliance recorder, transcribes in real time, and flags critical compliance breaches mid-call, before the rep finishes the pitch. One WebSocket protocol covers desk phones, soft-phones and video meeting tools.

Dialer streaming

First-class adapters for Twilio Media Streams and AWS Connect Voice Streams. Vonage, Plivo, Telnyx and any WebRTC dialer supported via our generic protocol. Older Genesys / Avaya stacks via SIPREC.

Browser audio streaming

Any modern browser can stream audio over the same WebSocket protocol. Useful for soft-phone agent desktops, embedded customer-facing forms, and reference clients you can drop into your own web app without writing the streaming layer.

Mid-call breach webhooks

HMAC-SHA256 signed POST to your endpoint within 30 seconds of detection. Retry with exponential backoff. Full audit log of every delivery attempt visible in your dashboard.

Confidence-gated alerts

Live scoring only emits a breach when the AI is >75% confident the criterion has clearly already failed, not "might be" or "could happen." Designed to avoid alert fatigue from day one.

Consent capture, audited

The streaming protocol takes a verbal-yes consent.captured event before scoring proceeds. Consent timestamps and excerpts are stored with the call and surfaced in the dashboard, giving you the GDPR Article 6 evidence trail without writing your own logic.

AI learning

An AI that gets better at your firm every week.

Generic AI scoring is wallpaper. CallGuard AI calibrates to your firm's interpretation of "suitable," "fair value" and "good outcome" using your team's own corrections, exemplars and coaching history. After 50 to 100 corrections the AI scores like your senior compliance reviewer, not a baseline that drifts.

Per-firm corrections feedback loop

Your compliance officer corrects the AI on borderline calls. Each correction becomes a few-shot example feeding the next batch of scoring. The AI learns "your fail" vs "industry fail" automatically.

Firm exemplar library

Mark gold-standard calls. The AI uses them as the reference bar when scoring future calls, so your firm's "good" defines the AI's "good," not someone else's.

AI Insights, written for your CO

A weekly strategic brief: what changed this week, the patterns worth attention, and 3 to 4 specific actions a compliance director will actually take. Written by Claude, not boilerplate.

Per-agent coaching memory

Coaching builds on what was said to that agent last week. If they've improved on the flagged area, the AI acknowledges it. If they haven't, it escalates the language. Personalised, not template.

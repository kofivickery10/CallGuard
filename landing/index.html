Browser audio streaming

Any modern browser can stream audio over the same WebSocket protocol. Useful for soft-phone agent desktops, embedded customer-facing forms, and reference clients you can drop into your own web app without writing the streaming layer.

Mid-call breach webhooks

HMAC-SHA256 signed POST to your endpoint within 30 seconds of detection. Retry with exponential backoff. Full audit log of every delivery attempt visible in your dashboard.

Confidence-gated alerts

Live scoring only emits a breach when the AI is >75% confident the criterion has clearly already failed, not "might be" or "could happen." Designed to avoid alert fatigue from day one.

Consent capture, audited

The streaming protocol takes a verbal-yes consent.captured event before scoring proceeds. Consent timestamps and excerpts are stored with the call and surfaced in the dashboard, giving you the GDPR Article 6 evidence trail without writing your own logic.

AI learning

An AI that gets better at your company every week.

Generic AI scoring is wallpaper. CallGuard AI calibrates to your company's interpretation of "suitable," "fair value" and "good outcome" using your team's own corrections, exemplars and coaching history. After 50 to 100 corrections the AI scores like your senior compliance reviewer, not a baseline that drifts.

Per-company corrections feedback loop

Your compliance officer corrects the AI on borderline calls. Each correction becomes a few-shot example feeding the next batch of scoring. The AI learns "your fail" vs "industry fail" automatically.

Exemplar call library

Mark gold-standard calls. The AI uses them as the reference bar when scoring future calls, so your company's "good" defines the AI's "good," not someone else's.

AI Insights, written for your CO

A weekly strategic brief: what changed this week, the patterns worth attention, and 3 to 4 specific actions a compliance director will actually take. Written by Claude, not boilerplate.

Per-agent coaching memory

Coaching builds on what was said to that agent last week. If they've improved on the flagged area, the AI acknowledges it. If they haven't, it escalates the language. Personalised, not template.

FAQ

Common questions, answered.

What is AI call QA and why does it matter?

AI call quality assurance uses speech recognition and large language models to transcribe and score every call your team takes against your scorecard, instead of a manual 5% sample done in spreadsheets. For contact centres and regulated businesses it means 100% coverage rather than statistical guesswork, breach detection in seconds rather than weeks, and systematic compliance evidence rather than anecdotes.

How does CallGuard AI score calls?

Each call is transcribed with speaker separation, then Claude scores it against your scorecard one criterion at a time. Every verdict is backed by a direct quote from the transcript so you can see why the AI passed or failed an item. Your knowledge base, prior compliance corrections and exemplar calls all feed into the prompt so the AI calibrates to your company's interpretation rather than a generic baseline.

Does it integrate with Twilio, AWS Connect or our existing dialer?

Yes. There are first-class adapters for Twilio Media Streams and AWS Connect Voice Streams. Other dialers can connect via our generic WebSocket protocol. You can also drag-and-drop recordings or push calls in via our REST API.

Can I display CallGuard results inside my own CRM or agent desktop?

Yes. Mint an API key in the dashboard, push calls in with a POST, then pull the result back per call. The result includes the score percentage, pass/fail per criterion, evidence quotes, breach list and the coaching draft. Render it however you want. Live streaming sessions also fire HMAC-signed webhooks on breach + scored events to your CRM or supervisor screen.

Does this support FCA Consumer Duty and the four outcomes?

Yes. CallGuard AI scores against your firm's own scorecard, which can encode COBS 9.2 suitability, FG21/1 vulnerable client indicators, charges and fair-value tests, and the four Consumer Duty outcomes (products and services, price and value, consumer understanding, consumer support). Every verdict is evidenced by a transcript quote, which is the audit trail the FCA expects.

How is customer data secured?

All audio is encrypted at rest with AES-256-GCM. Transport is TLS 1.2+. Access to the dashboard is JWT-authenticated with role-based access control. Data is hosted in the UK. A UK GDPR Article 28 Data Processing Agreement is available, and we publish our sub-processor list publicly so you know exactly who handles your data.

How long does it take to set up?

Sign up at 9am and have 100 calls scored before lunch. Each call is transcribed and scored in under a minute. There is no 90-day pilot, no solution architects, and no integration project. Upload an existing scorecard CSV, drop in any QA manuals or product cheatsheets to the knowledge base, and start scoring.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is per scored call with volume tiers, with the AI learning, exemplar library and coaching memory included on Growth and Pro plans. There is no per-seat licence fee. Get an exact quote against your call volume by emailing hello@callguardai.co.uk.

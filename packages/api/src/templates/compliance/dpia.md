## 1. Purpose of Processing

{{organization_name}} uses CallGuard, an AI-powered quality assurance platform, to transcribe and evaluate customer-facing call recordings against regulatory and internal quality standards. The purpose is to:

- Monitor ongoing compliance with applicable regulation (e.g. FCA Consumer Duty, COBS, MiFID II where applicable).
- Identify training and coaching needs for agents/advisers.
- Produce auditable records of call quality oversight.
- Evidence good client outcomes under Consumer Duty.

## 3. Lawful Basis

- **Article 6(1)(c)** - legal obligation (regulatory QA monitoring requirements).
- **Article 6(1)(f)** - legitimate interests (ensuring consumer protection and regulatory compliance).
- **Article 9(2)(g)** - where vulnerability indicators surface, processing is in the substantial public interest of protecting vulnerable consumers (supported by FG21/1).

## 4. Data Minimisation

- Only calls necessary for QA review are ingested.
- Transcripts are auto-redacted for payment card details where configured.
- Retention is limited to 7 years (aligned to COBS 9.5 / MiFID II record-keeping obligations).
- Access is role-limited: only Admins and designated Compliance users view full call content.

## 5. Technical & Organisational Safeguards

- **Encryption at rest**: all audio and documents encrypted with AES-256-GCM.
- **Encryption in transit**: TLS 1.3 minimum for all API connections.
- **Access control**: JWT authentication, role-based access (admin vs agent), row-level tenant isolation.
- **Audit trail**: all compliance actions (status changes, note additions) immutably logged.
- **UK data residency**: audio, transcripts, and metadata remain on UK-based servers.
- **Pseudonymisation**: internal IDs used in processing logs where possible.

## 6. Risks Identified & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|---|---|---|---|
| Unauthorised access to recordings | Low | High | Encryption + access controls + audit log |
| Misclassification leading to unfair coaching | Medium | Medium | Human review of all flagged calls before action |
| Over-retention of personal data | Low | Medium | Automated deletion after retention window |
| Cross-tenant data leakage | Very low | Very high | Row-level tenant isolation verified annually |
| AI hallucination in transcripts | Medium | Low | Evidence quotes linked to timestamps; human review required before FCA/HR action |

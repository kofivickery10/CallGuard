# Trust Point Mortgage and Protection Services — Company Overview

Trust Point Mortgage and Protection Services is a UK protection insurance
brokerage, **authorised and regulated by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA)**.

## What Trust Point does

- Advises on and arranges **protection insurance**: life cover, critical illness
  cover, and income protection.
- Works on a **fully advised basis** — across all the major insurers and lenders,
  to recommend cover suited to the client's needs.
- **Charges no fee** to the client for the advice service.

## How advice is delivered

Advisers work through a structured, fully-advised process on a recorded call:

1. **Intro** — introduce the adviser and Trust Point, explain the purpose of the
   call, and read the mandatory FCA regulatory statements.
2. **Fact find** — build a full picture of the client's circumstances, dependants,
   mortgage/debts, income and sick pay, existing cover, and any vulnerability.
3. **Mandatory services / vulnerability / data-sharing** statements, with explicit
   consent to share data with the insurer and verification provider.
4. **Needs summary, recommendation and quote** — confirmed with the client.
5. **Health & Lifestyle application** — the client answers all medical/lifestyle
   questions themselves, answered honestly and accurately.
6. **Wrap-up** — recap the recommendation, confirm the application outcome (accepted
   on-risk, or referred for underwriting), set up the direct debit, explain
   documents/cancellation rights/exclusions, and arrange any Trust and estate
   planning follow-up.

## Regulatory context

Trust Point operates under the FCA regime relevant to protection advice:
- **FCA authorisation & SM&CR** — evidence of compliance oversight of advice.
- **Consumer Duty (PS22/9)** — evidencing good client outcomes, including the
  duty-of-care / vulnerability handling.
- **ICOBS** — insurance conduct of business (suitability, disclosure, demands and
  needs).
- **Record retention** — call recordings and advice records retained for 5 years.

QA scoring for Trust Point should judge calls in the context of an
**FCA-regulated, fully-advised protection insurance sale**, where certain
regulatory statements must be delivered word-for-word and certain client
consents must be an explicit "yes".
